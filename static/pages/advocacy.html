Why We Need Real-Time Call Scanning

Voice deepfake technology has created a new generation of sophisticated scams targeting businesses and individuals. While our voicemail scanning technology provides valuable protection, real-time call scanning would offer immediate protection during live calls.

Unfortunately, current mobile platform restrictions prevent apps from accessing call audio in real-time for security analysis, even with user permission. This limitation significantly impacts our ability to protect users from voice scams as they happen.

Our Technology is Ready

We've already developed the technology for real-time call scanning, which can:

  • Analyze voice patterns during live calls
  • Detect synthetic or AI-generated voices in seconds
  • Alert users to potential scams before they share sensitive information
  • Operate with minimal battery impact and complete privacy

All we need now is for mobile platform providers to create secure APIs that allow security apps like ours to access call audio with explicit user permission.
