Installation Guide

Follow these simple steps to get VoiceGuardAI up and running on your device:

1

Download the App

Download VoiceGuardAI from the App Store or Google Play Store using the links above.

2

Install and Open

Once downloaded, the app will install automatically. Tap the VoiceGuardAI icon to open it.

3

Grant Permissions

When prompted, grant the necessary permissions for VoiceGuardAI to access your voicemails and audio files. These permissions are required for the app to analyze voicemails for potential scams.

4

Create an Account (Optional)

You can use VoiceGuardAI without an account, but creating one allows you to sync your scan history across devices and access all features.

5

Start Scanning

You're all set! You can now start scanning your voicemails for AI-generated voices and protect yourself from voice scams.
