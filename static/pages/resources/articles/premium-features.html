Premium Features Explained

Discover the advanced features and benefits of upgrading to VoiceGuardAI Premium

Last Updated: May 7, 2025
VoiceGuardAI Team

VoiceGuardAI offers both free and premium versions of our voice scam detection service. While the free version provides essential protection, upgrading to Premium unlocks a range of advanced features designed to enhance your security and improve your experience. This guide explains all the premium features in detail to help you decide if upgrading is right for you.

Premium Features Overview

Unlimited Scan History

Premium users can save and access their complete scan history without limitations. This allows you to keep track of all analyzed voicemails and their results indefinitely, making it easier to monitor patterns and identify recurring threats.

Free users are limited to viewing their 10 most recent scans, with older scan results being automatically deleted after 30 days.

Advanced Analysis Reports

Premium users receive detailed analysis reports that go beyond the basic classification. These reports include:

  • Comprehensive breakdown of acoustic features analyzed
  • Specific indicators that contributed to the classification
  • Confidence scores for individual detection metrics
  • Comparison with known voice scam patterns

These detailed reports help you better understand why a voicemail was flagged as suspicious and make more informed decisions about how to respond.

Priority Analysis

Premium users enjoy priority processing of their voicemail scans. During high-traffic periods, premium scans are placed at the front of the queue, ensuring faster results even when our system is experiencing heavy load.

This feature is particularly valuable when you need to quickly determine if a suspicious voicemail is legitimate before taking action.

Trusted Contacts Management

Premium users can create and manage a list of trusted contacts whose voicemails will automatically bypass scanning or receive special handling. This feature allows you to:

  • Add up to 50 trusted contacts (compared to 5 for free users)
  • Organize contacts into custom groups
  • Set different handling rules for different contacts or groups
  • Import contacts from your device's address book

Trusted contacts management helps reduce false positives and streamlines the process of handling voicemails from people you know and trust.

Customizable Detection Sensitivity

Premium users can fine-tune the sensitivity of VoiceGuardAI's detection algorithms to match their specific needs. This includes:

  • Adjusting the overall sensitivity level (Low, Medium, High, or Custom)
  • Setting custom thresholds for specific detection metrics
  • Creating different sensitivity profiles for different scenarios
  • Scheduling automatic sensitivity changes based on time or location

This level of customization allows you to balance security with convenience, reducing false positives while maintaining protection against genuine threats.

Advanced Notifications

Premium users have access to enhanced notification options, including:

  • Custom notification sounds for different scan results
  • Email notifications for suspicious voicemails
  • Scheduled notification summaries
  • Integration with third-party messaging apps
  • Emergency contact alerts for high-confidence scam detections

These advanced notification options ensure you never miss an important security alert, even when you're not actively using the app.

Priority Support

Premium users receive priority access to our support team, with guaranteed response times:

  • Email support responses within 4 hours during business hours
  • Live chat support during extended hours
  • Priority handling of bug reports and feature requests
  • Access to dedicated premium support specialists

Our priority support ensures that any issues or questions you have are addressed quickly and efficiently, minimizing disruption to your protection.

Cloud Backup & Sync

Premium users can securely back up their scan history, settings, and trusted contacts to the cloud, enabling:

  • Seamless synchronization across multiple devices
  • Easy recovery when changing or resetting devices
  • Automatic daily backups of all app data
  • Manual backup and restore options

Cloud backup and sync ensures you never lose your important security data and can maintain consistent protection across all your devices.

How to Upgrade

Upgrading to VoiceGuardAI Premium is quick and easy:

  1. Open the VoiceGuardAI app on your device
  2. Tap on your profile icon in the top-right corner
  3. Select "Upgrade to Premium"
  4. Choose your preferred subscription plan
  5. Complete the payment process using your App Store or Google Play account
Note: All new premium subscribers receive a 7-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial period ends, and you can cancel anytime during the trial without being billed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch between monthly and annual plans?

Yes, you can switch your subscription plan at any time. If you switch from monthly to annual, you'll immediately receive the discounted annual rate. If you switch from annual to monthly, the change will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Will I lose my data if I downgrade from Premium to Free?

If you downgrade from Premium to Free, you'll retain access to your 10 most recent scan results, but older scan history will no longer be accessible. Your custom settings will be reset to the free tier defaults, but you can reconfigure them if you upgrade again in the future.

Is there a family or group discount available?

Yes, we offer a Family Plan that covers up to 5 family members at a discounted rate. We also offer special pricing for businesses and organizations. Contact our sales team for details on group discounts.

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

To cancel your Premium subscription:

  • For iOS: Go to Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions > VoiceGuardAI > Cancel Subscription
  • For Android: Open Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > VoiceGuardAI > Cancel Subscription

Your Premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Pro Tip: If you're experiencing any issues with the app that are making you consider cancelling, please contact our support team first. We may be able to resolve the issue or offer an alternative solution that meets your needs.
