VoiceGuardAI offers a comprehensive set of advanced features designed to protect you from voice scams. All features are available to every user at no cost, as we believe voice security should be accessible to everyone. This guide explains all the advanced features in detail to help you get the most out of VoiceGuardAI.

Advanced Features Overview

Unlimited Voicemail Scanning

Scan as many voicemails as you need without any limits. Our advanced AI can analyze any number of voicemails to detect potential scams, giving you peace of mind with every message you receive.

Complete Scan History

Access your entire scan history at any time. VoiceGuardAI keeps a record of all your previous scans, allowing you to review past results and track patterns in scam attempts over time.

Detailed Analysis Reports

Get comprehensive reports for each scan, including risk factors, confidence scores, and specific indicators of potential scams. These detailed insights help you understand why a voicemail was flagged as suspicious.

Trusted Contacts List

Create a list of trusted contacts whose voicemails will be automatically verified. This helps reduce false positives and streamlines the scanning process for messages from people you know and trust.

Custom Notifications

Configure how and when you receive alerts about potential scams. Customize notification settings to suit your preferences and ensure you never miss important security alerts.

Priority Support

Access our dedicated support team whenever you need assistance. Our experts are available to help you with any questions or issues you may encounter while using VoiceGuardAI.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access these advanced features?

All features are available to every user immediately after installing and setting up the VoiceGuardAI app. There are no hidden fees or premium tiers - everything is included for free.

Are there any usage limits?

No, there are no limits on any features. You can scan as many voicemails as you need, store unlimited scan history, and use all advanced features without restrictions.

How is VoiceGuardAI able to offer all these features for free?

VoiceGuardAI is supported through grants, partnerships with organizations committed to fighting digital fraud, and community support. We believe that protection against voice scams should be accessible to everyone, which is why we've made all features available at no cost.

Will these features always remain free?

Yes, we are committed to keeping all VoiceGuardAI features free for all users. Our mission is to protect everyone from voice scams without any barriers.

Note: To get the most out of VoiceGuardAI, we recommend keeping your app updated to the latest version. This ensures you have access to the newest features and security improvements.
