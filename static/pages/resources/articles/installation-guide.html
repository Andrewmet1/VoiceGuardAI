Installation Guide

How to download and install VoiceGuardAI on iOS and Android devices

Last Updated: May 1, 2025
VoiceGuardAI Team

This guide will walk you through the process of downloading and installing VoiceGuardAI on your iOS or Android device. VoiceGuardAI is designed to be easy to install and use, with a straightforward setup process that gets you protected against voice scams in minutes.

System Requirements

Platform Minimum Requirements Recommended
iOS iOS 13.0 or later iOS 15.0 or later
Android Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later Android 10.0 or later
Storage 50 MB free space 100 MB free space
Internet Required for installation and analysis Wi-Fi recommended for updates
iOS Installation
Android Installation

1 Open the App Store

Locate and tap the App Store icon on your iOS device's home screen.

2 Search for VoiceGuardAI

Tap the Search tab at the bottom of the screen and enter "VoiceGuardAI" in the search bar at the top. Tap the Search button on your keyboard.

Tip: Make sure you're downloading the official VoiceGuardAI app. Look for our logo (a shield with a sound wave) and verify that the developer is listed as "VoiceGuard Technologies, Inc."

3 Download the App

Tap the "GET" button next to the VoiceGuardAI app. You may be prompted to authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password to confirm the download.

If you've previously downloaded the app, you'll see a cloud icon instead of "GET." Tap the cloud icon to re-download.

4 Wait for Installation

The app will begin downloading and installing automatically. You'll see a progress indicator on the app icon on your home screen.

Note: If you're not connected to Wi-Fi, you may see a prompt asking if you want to download using cellular data. If the app is larger than 200 MB, you'll need to connect to Wi-Fi or change your App Store settings to download using cellular data.

5 Open VoiceGuardAI

Once the installation is complete, tap "OPEN" in the App Store or find the VoiceGuardAI icon on your home screen and tap it to launch the app.

6 Grant Permissions

When you first open the app, you'll be prompted to grant several permissions that VoiceGuardAI needs to function properly:

  • Microphone Access: Required to analyze voicemails for AI-generated voices
  • Notifications: To alert you about scan results and potential threats
  • Storage Access: To access saved voicemail files for analysis

Tap "Allow" for each permission request. You can always adjust these permissions later in your device's Settings app.

Important: VoiceGuardAI requires these permissions to function properly. If you deny any of these permissions, some features may not work correctly.

Troubleshooting Installation Issues

App Won't Download

If you're having trouble downloading the app, try these solutions:

  • Check your internet connection: Make sure you have a stable Wi-Fi or cellular connection.
  • Check available storage: Ensure you have enough free space on your device.
  • Update your operating system: Make sure your device meets the minimum requirements listed above.
  • Restart your device: Sometimes a simple restart can resolve download issues.

App Crashes After Installation

If the app crashes immediately after installation:

  • Restart your device: This can clear temporary issues that might be causing crashes.
  • Reinstall the app: Uninstall VoiceGuardAI and then reinstall it from the App Store or Google Play Store.
  • Update your operating system: Make sure your device is running the latest version of iOS or Android.

Permission Issues

If you accidentally denied a permission that VoiceGuardAI needs:

  • For iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy > [Permission Type] > VoiceGuardAI and toggle the permission on.
  • For Android: Go to Settings > Apps > VoiceGuardAI > Permissions and grant the necessary permissions.
