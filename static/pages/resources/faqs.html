What is VoiceGuardAI?

VoiceGuardAI is an advanced mobile application that uses artificial intelligence to detect voice scams and protect you from fraudulent callers. Our technology analyzes voice patterns in real-time to identify potential scammers, keeping you and your loved ones safe from increasingly sophisticated voice scams.

How does VoiceGuardAI work?

VoiceGuardAI works by analyzing voice patterns during your phone calls. When you receive a call from an unknown number, our AI technology runs in the background to detect signs of synthetic or manipulated voices, as well as speech patterns commonly used by scammers. The app then provides a real-time risk assessment, alerting you if a call appears suspicious.

Which devices and operating systems are supported?

VoiceGuardAI is currently available for both iOS (iPhone) and Android devices. We support iOS 14.0 and above, and Android 8.0 (Oreo) and above. For optimal performance, we recommend using the latest version of your device's operating system.

Does VoiceGuardAI work with all phone calls?

VoiceGuardAI works with standard phone calls, VoIP calls, and most popular calling apps. However, some specialized calling applications or encrypted communication platforms may have limitations. The app is designed to work with your device's native calling system and common third-party calling apps like WhatsApp, Skype, and Zoom.

Will VoiceGuardAI drain my battery?

VoiceGuardAI is designed to be battery-efficient. It only activates during phone calls and uses optimized AI algorithms that run locally on your device. Our internal testing shows minimal battery impact—typically less than 5% additional battery usage per day with normal calling patterns. The app also includes a power-saving mode that you can enable in settings.

How accurate is the scam detection?

VoiceGuardAI has a high accuracy rate, with our latest model achieving over 95% accuracy in detecting synthetic or manipulated voices and 89% accuracy in identifying scam speech patterns. However, no system is perfect, and there may be false positives or missed detections. We continuously improve our AI models based on new data and user feedback to increase accuracy over time.

Does VoiceGuardAI record or store my calls?

No. VoiceGuardAI never records calls. It uses brief speakerphone audio — just 2-3 seconds — after you answer unknown numbers. All voice scans are user-initiated, brief, and processed securely. Your data is never recorded or stored without consent.

Is my data secure with VoiceGuardAI?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols. We implement strict access controls, regular security audits, and follow best practices for data protection. Our privacy-by-design approach means that we collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our service.

Is VoiceGuardAI GDPR compliant?

Yes, VoiceGuardAI is fully GDPR compliant. We respect the privacy rights of EU users and have implemented all necessary measures to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation. This includes providing transparent information about data processing, obtaining appropriate consent, implementing data minimization practices, and ensuring you can exercise your rights regarding your personal data. For more details, please see our GDPR Compliance page.

Do I have to use speakerphone?

Yes. That's how VoiceGuardAI picks up the caller's voice clearly, without capturing yours. When an unknown number calls, the app will prompt you to place the call on speakerphone before tapping the "Scan Caller" button. This ensures optimal voice detection accuracy.

Can it detect AI voices without me tapping anything?

No. Platform rules require you to tap "Scan Caller" — this ensures you're in control and that no background recording happens. VoiceGuardAI will never automatically record or analyze call audio without your explicit action. You are always in control of when scanning occurs.

Is VoiceGuardAI free to use?

VoiceGuardAI offers both free and premium tiers. The free tier includes 5 voice scans per month for unknown callers. Our premium subscription provides unlimited scans, auto-scan reminders, scan history, and number trust score caching. We offer a 14-day free trial of premium features so you can experience the full benefits before subscribing.

How much does the premium subscription cost?

Our premium subscription is available for $4.99/month or $49.99/year (saving you 17%). We also offer a per-scan bundle option: 20 scans for $4.99 if you prefer to pay only for what you use. All premium plans include unlimited scans, advanced detection algorithms, scan history, and priority customer support.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings in the app or through your app store's subscription management. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period. We don't offer partial refunds for unused subscription periods, but you won't be charged for the next billing cycle after cancellation.
