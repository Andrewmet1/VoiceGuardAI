What is VoiceGuardAI?

VoiceGuardAI is an advanced mobile application that uses artificial intelligence to detect voice scams and protect you from fraudulent callers. Our technology analyzes voicemails to identify synthetic or AI-generated voices, keeping you and your loved ones safe from increasingly sophisticated voice scams.

How does VoiceGuardAI work?

VoiceGuardAI works by analyzing voice patterns in voicemails. When you receive a suspicious voicemail, you can share it with our app, and our AI technology will detect signs of synthetic or manipulated voices. The app then provides a risk assessment, alerting you if a voicemail appears to be AI-generated or manipulated.

Which devices and operating systems are supported?

VoiceGuardAI is currently available for both iOS (iPhone) and Android devices. We support iOS 14.0 and above, and Android 8.0 (Oreo) and above. For optimal performance, we recommend using the latest version of your device's operating system.

Does VoiceGuardAI work with all voicemail formats?

VoiceGuardAI works with most standard voicemail formats, including those from mobile carriers, VoIP services, and popular messaging apps. You can share voicemails from your phone's native voicemail system or forward voice messages from apps like WhatsApp, Telegram, or other messaging platforms.

Will VoiceGuardAI drain my battery?

VoiceGuardAI is designed to be battery-efficient. Since it only activates when you manually share a voicemail for analysis, it has minimal impact on your device's battery life. The app uses optimized AI algorithms that process voicemails quickly and efficiently.

How accurate is the AI voice detection?

VoiceGuardAI has a high accuracy rate, with our latest model achieving over 95% accuracy in detecting synthetic or manipulated voices in voicemails. However, no system is perfect, and there may be false positives or missed detections. This technology is still brand new, and as AI continues to evolve, we need to adapt alongside it. While our system will never be 100% accurate, it provides an important line of defense to assist in identifying potential voice scams. We continuously improve our AI models based on new data and user feedback to increase accuracy over time.

Does VoiceGuardAI record or store my voicemails?

No. VoiceGuardAI never permanently stores your voicemails. When you share a voicemail with our app for analysis, it is processed immediately and then deleted from our servers. Only the analysis results are stored to help improve our detection algorithms. Your privacy is our top priority.

Is my data secure with VoiceGuardAI?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols. We implement strict access controls, regular security audits, and follow best practices for data protection. Our privacy-by-design approach means that we collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our service.

Is VoiceGuardAI GDPR compliant?

Yes, VoiceGuardAI is fully GDPR compliant. We respect the privacy rights of EU users and have implemented all necessary measures to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation. This includes providing transparent information about data processing, obtaining appropriate consent, implementing data minimization practices, and ensuring you can exercise your rights regarding your personal data. For more details, please see our GDPR Compliance page.

How do I share a voicemail with VoiceGuardAI?

Sharing a voicemail with VoiceGuardAI is simple. After receiving a suspicious voicemail, open the VoiceGuardAI app and tap the "Scan Voicemail" button. You'll be prompted to select the voicemail from your phone's voicemail system or to upload an audio file. Once selected, our AI will analyze the voicemail and provide you with results in seconds.

Is real-time call scanning available?

Currently, real-time call scanning is not available due to platform restrictions. While we have developed the technology, mobile platform policies currently prevent apps from accessing call audio in real-time for security analysis. We're advocating for changes to these policies to enable this critical security feature in the future.

Why is real-time call scanning important?

Real-time call scanning would provide immediate protection against voice scams as they happen. This would be especially valuable for vulnerable populations like the elderly, who are often targeted by scammers. With real-time scanning, users could be alerted to potential scams during the call, before they share sensitive information or fall victim to fraud.

You can support our advocacy efforts by signing our petition on the Advocacy page of our website. By adding your voice, you help us demonstrate to platform providers the strong public demand for better voice security tools. You can also share information about this initiative on social media to raise awareness about the importance of real-time voice scam protection.
