Common Issues & Solutions

Battery Drain Issues

Some users may experience higher than expected battery usage when running VoiceGuardAI in the background.

Solution:

  1. Update to the latest version of the app, which includes battery optimization improvements
  2. In the app settings, enable "Power Saving Mode" which reduces background processing
  3. Configure the app to only activate during calls rather than constant monitoring
  4. Check for other apps running in the background that might be contributing to battery drain

Microphone Access Problems

VoiceGuardAI requires microphone permissions to analyze call audio for scam detection.

Solution:

  1. Go to your device's Settings > Apps > VoiceGuardAI > Permissions
  2. Ensure microphone access is enabled
  3. For iOS: Settings > Privacy > Microphone > Enable for VoiceGuardAI
  4. For Android: Settings > Apps > VoiceGuardAI > Permissions > Microphone > Allow
  5. Restart your device after changing permissions

False Positive Detections

Sometimes VoiceGuardAI may incorrectly flag legitimate calls as potential scams.

Solution:

  1. Update to the latest version which includes improved detection algorithms
  2. Add frequent contacts to your "Trusted Callers" list in the app settings
  3. Adjust the sensitivity level in Settings > Detection Sensitivity
  4. Report false positives through the app to help improve our AI model
  5. Use the "Mark as Safe" feature when a legitimate call is incorrectly flagged

App Crashes or Freezes

If the app becomes unresponsive or crashes unexpectedly during use.

Solution:

  1. Force close the app and restart it
  2. Update to the latest version from your app store
  3. Clear the app cache (Android: Settings > Apps > VoiceGuardAI > Storage > Clear Cache)
  4. Ensure your device has sufficient free storage space
  5. If problems persist, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app
